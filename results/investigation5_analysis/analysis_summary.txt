INVESTIGATION 5: RADIUS-WEIGHTED TRAINING - ANALYSIS SUMMARY
================================================================================

HYPOTHESIS
----------
In spectral embeddings, high-radius nodes (||x||₂ large) are core cluster points,
while low-radius nodes are boundary/ambiguous points. Weighting the loss by radius
emphasizes confident training examples.

PREDICTION:
  - SIMPLE methods (no magnitude feature): Radius weighting should HELP
    → Magnitude signal is NEW information
  - COMPLEX methods (already use magnitude): Radius weighting should HURT
    → Magnitude used TWICE creates redundancy/interference
